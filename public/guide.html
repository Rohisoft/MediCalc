Update stock quantity

Tap any medicine card → a popup appears showing the current stock → enter the new quantity → tap "Update Stock".

4

Add a new medicine

Tap "+ Add New" in the top-right → fill in Name, Category, Price, Stock Quantity, Unit, and Expiry date → tap "Save Medicine".

⚠️ Stock alertsBelow 10 units = yellow (Low). Zero units = red (Out of Stock). Expiry within 90 days = orange warning. Expired = red. These also appear on your Dashboard alerts.

6

Pay Later / Due Amount

1

Create a Pay Later bill

In the New Bill screen, add medicines → tap the green cart bar → in the review screen select "Pay Later 📒" as payment method.

2

Select the customer

Tap the 👤 Customer row in the review screen and select who will pay later. A customer must be selected for Pay Later bills.

3

Complete the bill

The bill amount is added to the customer's Due balance shown as a red badge on their customer card.

4

Collect payment when they return

Go to Customers tab → tap their name → tap "Collect Payment" → enter amount → confirm.

Due Balance: ₹850

7

Sharing a Bill to Customer

1

Open Bill History

In the New Bill screen, tap "📋 History" at the top right.

2

Tap a bill to open the receipt

You'll see the full receipt with all items, totals, GST, and your shop details.

3

Tap "📤 Share Receipt"

A formatted receipt text is generated. On Android/iPhone, tap WhatsApp to send it directly to the customer.
